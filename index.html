About Mandy

I’m a freelance writer, editor and fact-checker based in Moncton, New Brunswick, Canada. I write about travel, food and music. I’ve also recently started working as a front-end web developer.

Bylines include WestJet Magazine, Canadian Geographic Magazine, mental_floss.com and more. I've fact-checking magazines like OnBoard, This Magazine, Dementia Connections, Leap Magazine and others.

In my spare time, I write short fiction. I’ve been long-listed twice in the Australian Writers’ Centre’s Furious Fiction competition. I was also a finalist in NYC Midnight’s 2021 micro-fiction contest.

I have a combined honours degree in Journalism and Music History from the University of King’s College in Halifax, Nova Scotia.

When I’m not working, I’m travelling, baking, coding, kickboxing or listening to new wave.
